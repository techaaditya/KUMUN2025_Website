Awards & Recognition

Outstanding Delegate

Recognized for exceptional diplomatic skills, leadership, and contribution to committee discussions.

Criteria:

  • Active participation in debates
  • Quality of speeches and interventions
  • Collaboration and consensus-building
  • Understanding of country position

Honorable Mention

Awarded to delegates who demonstrate strong diplomatic skills and meaningful contributions.

Criteria:

  • Consistent participation
  • Effective communication
  • Respect for parliamentary procedure
  • Positive committee atmosphere

Best Position Paper

Recognizes delegates who submit exceptional position papers demonstrating thorough research.

Criteria:

  • Comprehensive research
  • Clear country position
  • Proposed solutions
  • Professional writing style

Rules & Procedures

KUMUN 2025 follows the United Nations Rules of Procedure, adapted for educational purposes. All delegates are expected to familiarize themselves with these rules before the conference.

Parliamentary Procedure

Formal debate structure with motions, points, and voting procedures

Dress Code

Business formal attire required for all committee sessions

Electronic Devices

Laptops and tablets permitted for research and note-taking

Language

English is the official language of the conference
